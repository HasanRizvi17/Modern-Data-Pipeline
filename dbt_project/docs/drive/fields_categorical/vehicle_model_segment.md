### Vehicle Model Segment
Classifies vehicles into pricing and comfort segments.

### Possible Values
| Segment | Meaning |
|--------|--------|
| `economy` | Entry-level vehicles |
| `comfort` | Mid-range vehicles |
| `premium` | High-end vehicles |

### Business Rules
- Segment is assigned at vehicle model creation
- Segment influences pricing and package availability

### Usage Notes
- Use segment for pricing and demand analysis
- Compare utilization across segments
